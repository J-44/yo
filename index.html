CLIMATE IN TROPICAL EVERGREEN FOREST


Climate in tropical evergreen forest is wet and humid because of heavy rainfall , the average annual temperature is between 25°C and 27°C and average annual humidity exceeds 77 percent in tropical evegreen forest.
